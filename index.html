About Our Studio

Welcome to Sanskriti Handicraft Studio, nestled in the vibrant heart of Koregaon Park, Pune. We bring the beauty of handcrafted artistry into your daily life — one piece at a time.

Founded by Sanskriti Pawar, our studio celebrates traditional craftsmanship fused with modern aesthetics. Every item is unique, thoughtfully designed, and tells a story of passion and heritage.

From luminous resin art to delicate crochet, each creation is made by hand with love and intention — never mass produced.
